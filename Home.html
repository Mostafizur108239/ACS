About Us

ACS (Annual Charity Show) is a small charity which looks after animals that need extra support. Each year ACS promotes an annual charity show in the community to help raise additional funds for the different animal shelters it has around the country. We are looking for those people who arent able to fosterage the animals. On the other hand, people who are interested to donate for the animals, we accept them. We give shelters for the helpless animal for a long time. We provide safesty for the animals also. So, we are the group of helping hands to ensure a well life for the animals.

We are introduce some social works about animals. Animals are so important for our society. So we need to protect the animals!
