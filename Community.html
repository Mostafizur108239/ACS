Annual Charity Show

As part of their Skills Project, the Animal Care students are running a charity dog show between 11am – 12:30pm. Dogs and their owners are invited to enter the competition, all proceeds raised will go to the National Animal Welfare Trust. Categories include: dog most like its owner, best puppy, best senior, best groomed, best crossbreed, best talent and best in show. The cost to enter each category is £1.50. The top three dogs in each category win a prize.
